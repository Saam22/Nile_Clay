Our Story

Where Heritage Meets Innovation

Founded in 2010 by Chef Saad Hassan, Nile & Clay brings the rich culinary traditions of Egypt to the modern table. Our philosophy is simple: honor the ingredient, respect the tradition, and create something extraordinary.

Every dish tells a story — of the Nile Delta farms where we source our vegetables, of the fishermen who bring us the freshest catch, and of generations of Egyptian home cooks whose recipes inspire us daily.
